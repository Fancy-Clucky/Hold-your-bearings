What is Hold your Bearings?

Hold Your Bearings was started as many groups do. A group of friends wanting to expand their hobby socially and skillfully. We created Hold Your Bearings as a way to get new individuals into the hobby and make it easier for them. When we accomplish this we also gain new active members of the community and larger events. We hope to continue to grow and expand our community, but our top priority is safety and just having fun.
